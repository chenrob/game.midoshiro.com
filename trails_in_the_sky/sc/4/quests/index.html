Sidequest: Missing Engagement Ring

Enter the Rolent Church and talk to TODO at TODO. A bird has stolen Arumu and Aiali's engagement ring. Head over to the Esmelas Tower and ascend all the way to the top to find it. Once the ring is recovered, head back to Rolent Church to return it.

You can head to the Esmelas Tower now or later in the game. You'll find the ring at the top of the tower. After that, go back to Rolent Church to return the ring.

Reward

BP+4, $5000

Sidequest: Discover Fishing Sites

Head to Grune Gate and talk to Paiser to find out that the Fisherman Guild is looking for fishing spots in the Rolent area.

To successfully "record" a fishing spot, Estelle must actually fish out a fish from the spot. Since the last fishing spot is inaccessible until the end of the chapter, Estelle can take her leisurely time recording fishing spots as she encounters them. The fishing spots are as follows:

Advice You don't need to "go out of your way" to record all the fishing spots now (especially since the last one is only accessible at the end of the chapter). You can record the fishing spots as you progress through the story -- the fishing spots will be highlighted in the walkthrough.
  1. Welter Bridge Pass
  2. Milch Main Road Pond
  3. Beside the bridge at Elize Road
  4. Behind the Bright House
  5. Rolent Sewers
  6. Near the Big Tree in the Mysterious Forest
    Note: this fishing spot is accessible only after defeating the chapter boss

When you're done, head back to Grune Gate to report to Paiser.

Reward

Sidequest: Find the Lost Cat

This is the Trails in the Sky SC version of a similar sidequest in Trails in the Sky FC.

Unconfirmed: if you solve this sidequest with Olivier and Kloe, you'll get additional dialogue between those two.

Head to the Rolent Inn and enter the room in front of the entrance and talk to Yina. Yina took a nap and her cat went missing ... again.

Head to the airport, go upstairs, and talk to the guy beside the airbridge. Then go down to the warehouse area at the back of the airport and talk to the guy in the alley between the two buildings.

Leave the airport and talk to Quint in the Orbment Workshop. Go back to the airport and talk to the guy at the southwest tree. Then go back upstairs and let Scherazard "convince" the guy to let you in the airship (LOL). Head down to the end of the cargo hold to find .... lots of cats!

Sidequest: Anti-Drunk Secret Drug

Find Anton at the top of the clock tower (you might need to rotate the map a bit to find the back door to the clock tower). He knows of a secret drug that negates the effect of alcohol, so he plans to ask Aina out on a date by challenging her to a drinking contest.

But in order to make this anti-drunk drug, he needs Estelle to help him get the following ingredients:

  • Monster Shell (魔獸甲殼) x5
    (甲殼珊瑚)
  • Monster Wing (魔獸羽翼) x5
    (紅茶鉗蟲、吸血蚊)
  • Monster Seed (魔獸之種) x5
    (爆種鈴蘭、大食人花)
  • Pearlgrass Fish (珍珠草) x1

Estelle should have accumulated enough of the monster body parts from fighting in random battles. The Pearlgrass fish can be found at the Ailitz Road fishing spot (south of Rolent, beside the bridge). Since fishing is a chance thing, it would be wise to save before fishing so you don't waste too much bait.

For the highest probability of actually fishing out a Pearlgrass fish, Estelle should use the Conch (田螺) bait with the Bamboo Rod (竹竿).

If you didn't listen to me in the Chapter 3 walkthrough, theeeeeeeeoretically you can also use the Worm bait with the standard Fishing Rod.

When Estelle finishes collecting all the items, return to Rolent and give it to Anton. Now sit back and watch him challenge Aina at a drinking contest (it's quite hilarious).

Reward

BP+2, $7500

Sidequest: Recover Mining Permit

Blblanc is at it again. And yes, again it's another set of cryptic messages left clue cards.

Estelle starts off with the first clue, the "person sitting beside the Goddess". Enter the Septium Church and head to the upstairs balcony. Inspect the empty chair.

The next clue is a diagram. Leave the church and exit the map east to the Mayor's home. Look behind his house (might need to rotate the map a bit) for a set of boxes stacked like the diagram, three at the bottom and two at the top.

The last clue is the "eight-eyed metal monster". Return back to the main Rolent map and go to the rear of the weapon store. Investigate the furnace door (again, rotate the map) for a rusted key.

Enter the Rolent Sewers (it's behind the church) and use the rusted key to unlock the door at the end of the sewers. Save and enter for a boss fight.
