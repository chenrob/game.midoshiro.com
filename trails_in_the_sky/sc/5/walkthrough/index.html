Don't forget to go back to Nebel Valley and take the right path to find a log cabin where you can learn Dark Hot Pot - Realize (黑暗火鍋 - 覺悟). This is quite a useful recipe -- it sets one party member's HP and CP to 0, but gives +100 CP to everyone else.
