Deck

Once Estelle reaches the deck, she will soon encounter a forced battle with bracer points at stake. You should equip her with accessories that can prevent "loss of control" status effects like Faint.

Also, depending also on how diligently you followed this walkthrough, you may also want to adjust Estelle's quartz (especially if you didn't or weren't able to cook all those Fainting Meatballs (昏厥肉丸子)) so she can cast some higher level spells. Played conventionally, this is perhaps one of the most difficult battles in the game (up to this point). Estelle is fighting alone, outnumbered 5 to 1, and faces a time limit. Unless you grinded her to something ridiculous like level 70+, it's near impossible to win this battle "normally". So we should resort to some cheap tactics.

By the way, in the interests of full disclosure, I didn't come up with these tactics. They're translated from other readers' comments on a Chinese walkthrough I used when playing this game. Here are my thoughts on each tactic:

Tactic 1: Attack with Food

This is my preferred tactic and makes this battle really easymode. If you followed the walkthrough diligently, you should have a small arsenal of Fainting Meatballs (昏厥肉丸子). This is probably one of the best foods to use because:

  • It is currently your highest damaging food item (900 STR)
  • It has a 100% chance of causing Faint (fewer enemies attacking Estelle)
  • Its range is a pretty long line (can strike enemies afar)

Basically, all you do is throw meatballs at everyone. Whenever it is Estelle's turn, throw a meatball at the closest non-disabled enemy. Two or three meatballs should be able to take out anybody.
